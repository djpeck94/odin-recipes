Steak Recipe

This simple steak recipe delivers a juicy, flavorful cut with a perfect sear. Ideal for beginners or experienced cooks alike, it's a reliable method whether you're cooking for a special dinner or just craving a great piece of meat. This version uses a cast iron skillet to get a golden crust while keeping the inside tender.

Ingredients

For the steak:

  • 1 boneless ribeye or New York strip steak (about 1-inch thick)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil (or other high-smoke-point oil)
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 garlic cloves, smashed
  • 2-3 sprigs fresh thyme or rosemary (optional)

Steps to Cook

  1. Let the steak come to room temperature. Remove the steak from the fridge about 30-60 minutes before cooking. This helps it cook more evenly.
  2. Season generously. Pat the steak dry with paper towels. Season both sides liberally with salt and pepper.
  3. Preheat the skillet. Place a cast iron skillet over high heat. Let it heat up for about 5 minutes, until it's very hot.
  4. Sear the steak. Add oil to the pan, then place the steak in the skillet. Let it cook undisturbed for 2-3 minutes until a golden crust forms.
  5. Flip and sear the other side. Turn the steak and sear the second side for another 2-3 minutes.
  6. Baste with butter and aromatics. Add butter, smashed garlic, and herbs to the skillet. Tilt the pan slightly and use a spoon to baste the melted butter over the steak repeatedly for 1-2 minutes.
  7. Check for doneness. Use a meat thermometer to check internal temp: 125째F for rare, 135째F for medium-rare, 145째F for medium. Remove from heat when it's 5째F below your target, as it will continue cooking.
  8. Let it rest. Transfer steak to a plate and let it rest uncovered for 5-10 minutes. This helps the juices redistribute for maximum tenderness.
  9. Slice and serve. Slice against the grain and serve immediately. Optionally, top with a bit of finishing salt or serve with your favorite sauce.
